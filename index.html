About Me

My name is Jeremiah Gaiser, a PhD student at the university of Montana. While officially a student of the Biochemistry and Biophysics department at the UM, I am also deeply involved in the department of computer science.

Research Interests

I'm interested in just about everything in the realm of computational biology. Currently, my research goals are in the development of machine learning models for predicting protein-ligand binding affinity. At the moment, I'm pretty enthusiastic about deep learning, statistical learning theory, protein dynamics, and sequence analysis.
